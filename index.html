Heat and wildfire reated discomfort, ill-health, and death will only increase with climate change. These effects are increased for people who are unable to change their environmental circumstances due to the physical incapacitation that comes with incarceration. This map visualizes where the negative effects of climate change related heat and wildfire will be most pronounced in the U.S. This tool allows you to explore the ways that people incarcerated for mental health issues, for being in the wrong country, and who have been captured by the carceral state will only experience increasing levels of ill-health and human-rights abuses.
